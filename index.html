I Thesis

We invest and build at the seam where code meets concrete, where compliance shapes commerce, and where the combination of execution and capital is the moat.

01 / Artificial Intelligence

Models that compound.

Real value sits with operators who can deploy AI inside complex environments, against a new operating truth. Humans and AI workers building institutional knowledge together, through exit.

02 / Physical Industry

Atoms that still matter.

Logistics, manufacturing, field services. Workflows where the physical layer makes the moat: hard to replicate, hard to disrupt, expensive to ignore.

03 / Regulated Markets

Rules that create scarcity.

Where the right to operate is the asset. Licenses, accreditations, and audited track records take years to build and a single breach to lose. We back the firms that treat regulation as a moat.

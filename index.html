I Thesis

We invest at the seam where code meets concrete, where compliance shapes commerce, and where execution, not capital, is the moat.

01 / Artificial Intelligence

Models that compound.

Real value sits with operators who can deploy AI inside complex environments, not vendors selling licenses. Every Ancrage company has a path to running on AI workers from day one of our hold.

02 / Physical Industry

Atoms that still matter.

Logistics, manufacturing, energy, healthcare, freight, mining, construction. Workflows where the physical layer makes the moat: hard to replicate, hard to disrupt, expensive to ignore.

03 / Regulated Markets

Rules that create scarcity.

Healthcare, financial services, defense, utilities, life sciences. Compliance is a barrier to entry for the careless and a compounding asset for the disciplined operator.

Talent is everywhere.
Opportunity isn't.

We invest globally. North America is home base. Asia and the Gulf are structural. We do not need an opportunity to live within a postal code we already know.

II Mandate

What we do, what we don't.

Ancrage backs companies where AI, physical industry, and regulation meet. Control buyouts when the situation calls for it. JVs, growth equity, and structured deals when those are the right shape. The hold is long, the playbook is operational, and the leverage is the forward-deployed model we install alongside the team that built the company.

We are not a quarterly fund. We are not a fee aggregator. We are not interested in financial engineering as a substitute for execution.

Enterprise Value USD $25M to $100M
